A Descriptive Analysis of Impact Categories in Startup Ecosystems

by Nils Ness

About

What is Impact? That's exactly the main Research Question of my thesis. But how could be measured what impact is? In my thesis I collected the data from 200 Intermediarie programs that have specialized themselves to support young ventures and Startups to ceate an impact on the planet and the humans living on it. I have selected the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) as an orientation for the goals that could fall into the area of impact. The 17 SDGs are formally defined to achieve a more sustainable and better world until the year of 2030 by the United Nations in 2015. In the process of data collection I identified to which goals the intermediaries commit themselves. And after the collection process I analyzed the collected data to find out if clusters of targets could be found that occur together more often. To identify this common occurrence, I have plotted the correlation between the 17 SDGs in a correlation matrix. I then filtered the results to display the data relevant to my thesis. First, I checked the significance of the individual values and faded out all with a p-value > 0.05. Then I faded out all values with a very low correlation to show only the values with a correlation above 0.04.
